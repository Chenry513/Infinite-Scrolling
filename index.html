Design Interventions

UPWARD COMPARISON (Paper 1)
Reality check - shows creator's resources/team
Paid/Edited badges - transparency on produced content
Mood check-in - asks how user is feeling
Regular creator badge - highlights non-influencer content
ATTENTION & SELF-CONTROL (Paper 2)
Ad breaks - forced pause every 30 videos
Desaturation - colors fade over time
Longer videos - algorithm shifts to 2-3 min content
